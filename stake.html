STAKING

Genesis Pandas can be staked to earn $BAMBOO, an ERC-20 utility token used in the Expandables ecosystem. Staking involves depositing your Pandas into a separate contract, at which point they will begin to generate 25 $BAMBOO per day. Pandas can be unstaked at any time.
